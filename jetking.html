Master in Blockchain Development

Blockchain is a system of recording information in a way that makes it difficult or impossible to change, hack, or cheat the system. ... Each block in the chain contains a number of transactions, and every time a new transaction occurs on the blockchain, a record of that transaction is added to every participant's ledger. The number of live blockchains is growing every day, and at an ever-increasing pace. As of 2021, there are more than 10,000 active cryptocurrencies based on blockchain, with several hundred more non-cryptocurrency blockchains.
